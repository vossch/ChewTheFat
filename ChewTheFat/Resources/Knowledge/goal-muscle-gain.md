summary: Calorie-surplus guidance for lean gains.

---

Lean gain rates: 0.2–0.45 kg per week (or ~0.5 % of bodyweight). Faster
than that drives more fat than muscle.

Daily calorie target ≈ `TDEE + (weeklyChangeKg × 7700) / 7`.

Macro defaults during a surplus: protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg body weight, fat
0.7–1.0 g/kg, carbs fill the remainder (carbs fuel training).

Pair the surplus with progressive resistance training to bias the gain
toward lean mass.
