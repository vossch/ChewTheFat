Energy density:

- Protein: 4 kcal/g
- Carbohydrate: 4 kcal/g
- Fat: 9 kcal/g
- Alcohol: 7 kcal/g
- Fiber: ~2 kcal/g (subset of carbs)

General targets for adults:

- Protein: 0.8–2.2 g/kg body weight (lower for sedentary, higher for
  trainees and during a deficit).
- Fat: 0.6–1.0 g/kg body weight; never < 0.4 g/kg sustained.
- Carbs: fill the remaining calories; concentrate around training.
- Fiber: 25 g (women) / 38 g (men) per day.

When the user asks about a specific food, prefer values returned by
`food_search` over generic ranges in this file.
