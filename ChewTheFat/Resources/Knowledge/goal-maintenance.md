title: Maintenance
summary: Eat at TDEE for body composition stability.
tags: maintenance, tdee, macros
---

Daily calorie target = TDEE (BMR × activity multiplier).

Macro defaults: protein 1.4–1.8 g/kg, fat 0.8–1.0 g/kg, carbs fill the
remainder. Weekly weight change target should be 0 kg.

Reassess monthly: if weight drifts by more than ±1 % across four weeks,
nudge calories ±100 kcal/day to stabilise.
