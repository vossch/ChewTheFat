title: Weight Loss
summary: Calorie-deficit guidance for sustainable fat loss.

---

A safe sustained loss is 0.25–0.7 kg per week. 1 kg of body fat ≈ 7 700 kcal.
Therefore weekly deficit (kcal) ≈ `weeklyChangeKg × 7700`.

Daily calorie target ≈ `TDEE − weeklyDeficit / 7`.

Macro defaults during a deficit: protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg body weight, fat
0.6–1.0 g/kg, carbs fill the remainder. Higher protein protects lean mass.

Never recommend below 1 200 kcal/day for women or 1 500 kcal/day for men
without a medical caveat.
